About SchoolConnect

Student Assistant is a simple tool designed to help students stay organized with homework reminders, notes, and study timers. It helps students manage their time better and keep track of their school work.

Why We Created It

Many students forget homework or struggle to organize their study time. Student Assistant was created to make studying easier and help students stay focused and prepared for school.

Creator

Student Assistant was created by Madhav Navaneeth, a student who enjoys learning programming and building useful tools for students.

What You Can Do

  • Set homework reminders
  • Use a study timer
  • Write and save notes
  • Stay organized with school tasks

Our goal is to make studying simpler and help students build good study habits while staying organized.
